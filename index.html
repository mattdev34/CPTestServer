Activities

Play minigames to earn coins and stamps, or host parties in your igloo with an always growing number of things to take part in on Club Penguin Journey!

Parties

Special events which change the look of the island, give new tasks to complete, and have items to unlock happen every month! Who knows what the next one will be?

Community

Club Penguin Journey has an active and helpful community, which is growing larger every day! Each week, a penguin is selected for rewards who has stood out from the crowd!

Find Your Style

With new clothing items and catalogs releasing all the time, you can choose what you want to wear! Be a pirate, astronaut or alien, whatever you dream, you can be it!
